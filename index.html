01 — Problem

AI tools today are broken.

i.
Tab chaos

30+ tabs open, zero context between them. Every new AI chat starts from scratch and you repeat yourself endlessly.

ii.
Context resets

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — every session is a blank slate. You re-explain your role, your project, your preferences. Every. Single. Time.

iii.
Blind AI

AI assistants work in isolation. They cannot see your screen, open tabs, or what you're doing. The context gap is enormous.

iv.
No real action

LLMs give answers but can't fill forms, send Slack messages, create events, or push to GitHub. The last mile is broken.

02 — Solution

Meet Lyto AI.

one
Persistent
context memory

Lyto remembers everything across tabs and sessions. No more re-explaining. It learns your workflow over time and surfaces what matters.

two
DOM interaction

Reads and acts on any page element. Fill forms, extract data, navigate pages — completely hands-free on any website.

three
Eight deep integrations

Google Docs, Sheets, Calendar, Gmail, WhatsApp, Slack, GitHub, Figma — all natively connected and actionable from a single prompt.

four
Research mode

Verified answers with visual graphs and 3D data objects. Deep research built directly into your browser — no tab-switching required.
